## The specific invention

Athens did not invent political life. Athens did not invent
civic argument, the city-state, or popular participation in
government — each of these existed in the wider ancient
Mediterranean world in some form before Athens. What Athens
invented, across the sixth, fifth and fourth centuries BCE,
was the specific working *combination*: a city-state whose
constitutional form gave the working power of political
decision to a citizen body large enough to require deliberation
in public, supported by institutional procedures that made
the deliberation orderly, and accompanied by a sustained
intellectual tradition that *reflected on the practice while
the practice was occurring*.

This is the practice the European tradition has continued to
read as the founding case of self-government conducted in
public.

## The constitutional stages

The Athenian constitutional development across two centuries
is unusually well documented for an ancient polity. The
working stages:

*Solon* in 594 BCE — the working start of the constitutional
differentiation. The four census classes by property; the
cancellation of debt-bondage (*seisachtheia*); the working
admission of the lower census classes to the Heliaia
(popular court); the foundation of the Boulē of 400. Solon's
reforms did not produce democracy in any later sense; they
produced the *conditions* under which democracy would become
possible.

*Cleisthenes* in 508 BCE — the working production of the
democratic form. The reorganisation of the citizen body into
ten artificial *phylai* (tribes) drawn from the three
regional *trittyes* (city, coast, inland) of Attica. The
working effect: the political units across which citizens
deliberated were no longer the inherited family/aristocratic
units but artificial cross-regional groupings designed to
prevent factional concentration. The Boulē of 500, rotated by
lot through the citizen body annually, became the working
preparatory body of the demos.

*Ephialtes and Pericles* in the mid-fifth century — the
working completion of the form. Paid public service so the
working poor could participate; popular juries with authority
over the magistrates; sortition (allotment) rather than
election for most offices; the strengthening of the
*Ekklēsia* as the sovereign deliberative body; the working
opening of the polity's principal institutions to the
political class the navy had empowered.

The form continued to operate, with the Peloponnesian-War
disruption and the two oligarchic interludes of 411 and 404
BCE, until the Macedonian absorption following Chaeronea in
338 BCE. The Athenian *democratic* practice survived in
working form for the better part of two centuries; the
*polis* form continued under Macedonian and then Roman
hegemony for centuries more, with the working constitutional
content increasingly attenuated.

## What the form actually did

The working Athenian democratic practice produced specific
outcomes the European tradition has continued to read.

It produced *the practice of holding political life
accountable through public argument*. Magistrates were
*euthynai* (audited) at the end of their year; major political
figures could be prosecuted by *eisangelia* (public
impeachment); the assembly could change its own previous
decisions when persuaded to (the Mytilenean debate is the
canonical case). The working accountability was real, if
sometimes harsh — the strategoi who survived Arginusae in
406 BCE were tried and executed in mass by an assembly that
turned against them.

It produced *the institutional rotation of political work*
across the citizen body. The Boulē's annual lot meant that a
large fraction of adult male citizens served on the council
during a working lifetime. The popular courts gave the same
practice to the judicial side. The system was not designed
to produce *expert* administration in the modern sense; it
was designed to produce *broad* civic competence.

It produced *the cultural confidence* that the city's
political affairs were the citizens' affair. Pericles's
*Funeral Oration* (Thucydides II.36-46) is the working
ancient statement of this confidence at full operation.

It also produced *the working failures* the classical sources
record without flinching. The Mytilenean reversal; the
Sicilian expedition; the working pressure under which the
assembly approved the death of Socrates; the periodic
oligarchic counter-revolutions. The form did not, by itself,
guarantee the working substrate of judgement it depended on.

## The intellectual reflection inside the form

What is structurally unusual about the Athenian case is that
the *theoretical reflection on the practice* happened *inside*
the practice. Socrates conducted his public ethical
examinations in the same agora the political life of the
city happened in; Plato founded the Academy a generation
after the trial of Socrates and wrote the philosophical
dialogues partly *as a working critique of the polity that
condemned his teacher*; Aristotle wrote the <cite>Politics</cite>
as a working systematic analysis of the constitutional forms
he had observed across the Greek world. The reflection and
the practice were the same working culture.

The Athenian tradition is therefore not simply *the working
democracy* — it is *the working democracy together with the
philosophical critique of the working democracy*. The two
sides of the Athenian inheritance, the practical and the
philosophical, are not separable. The European political
tradition received both.

## Why the platform reads it

The platform reads Athens because the working substrate of
constitutional self-government in the European tradition is
unintelligible without the Athenian case. The institutional
vocabulary, the practice of public argument, the philosophical
examination of what political life is for, the
historiographical practice of pragmatic causal analysis —
each is a working Athenian invention the European tradition
has continued to use. The careful reading is that Athens
produced the most considered working ancient experiment in
self-government, that the experiment achieved specific things
no other ancient polity matched, and that the experiment had
specific structural costs and exclusionary working conditions
the platform reads at the centre rather than at the margin.
