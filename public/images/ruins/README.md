# Ruins

preserved civilizational fabric where the surviving material is the
record. Vendored locally; provenance recorded here and in the typed
registry at
[`src/data/archive-images.ts`](../../../src/data/archive-images.ts).

## Catalog

### `roman-forum-view.webp`

- **Subject.** Overview of the Roman Forum, looking east. Structures
  spanning the Republican and imperial periods — the Arch of
  Septimius Severus, the Curia Julia (Augustan rebuild), the Temple
  of Antoninus and Faustina, and the Palatine Hill behind.
- **Location.** Rome, Italy.
- **Source.** Wikimedia Commons,
  [`File:Forum Romanum Rome 5.jpg`](https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Forum_Romanum_Rome_5.jpg).
- **Photographer.** Jebulon.
- **Licence.** **CC-Zero (public-domain dedication).**
- **Source dimensions.** 3096 × 2273.
- **Local processing.** Resized to 1600 px on the longest edge with
  `sharp`, encoded as WebP at quality 80, effort 6. Resulting file is
  ~468 KB at 1600 × 1175.
