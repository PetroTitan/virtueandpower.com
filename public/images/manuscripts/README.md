# Manuscripts

Manuscript fragments, scribal pages and early printed editions — the
physical vehicles through which the texts the platform reads were
transmitted. Vendored locally; provenance recorded here and in the typed
registry at
[`src/data/archive-images.ts`](../../../src/data/archive-images.ts).
Dropping a file here is not enough — it must also be registered there.

## Catalog

### `plutarch-lives-manuscript.webp`

- **Subject.** A two-column page of a Byzantine Greek manuscript of
  Plutarch's Parallel Lives — one of the medieval copies through which
  the text survived to the Renaissance and the modern world.
- **Location.** Bodleian Library, Oxford (MS. Canonici Greek 93).
- **Source.** Wikimedia Commons,
  [`File:Plutarch, Parallel Lives, Oxford, MS. Canonici Greek 93 (cropped).jpg`](https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Plutarch,_Parallel_Lives,_Oxford,_MS._Canonici_Greek_93_(cropped).jpg).
- **Holder.** Bodleian Library.
- **Licence.** **Public domain** (faithful reproduction of a manuscript
  out of copyright by age).
- **Source dimensions.** 2298 × 1786.
- **Local processing.** Resized to 1600 px longest edge, WebP q80 e6;
  ~227 KB at 1600 × 1244. Vendored for the Parallel Lives hub.

## Adding new manuscript images

Same pipeline as the architecture directory. Verify the rights status,
resize to 1600 px longest edge, encode WebP q80 e6, register in
`src/data/archive-images.ts`, and add an entry here. No AI imagery; no
uncertain attributions.
